Miscellania is a small mod with a whole bunch of random ideas. There is no specific theme for the items.

Current features include:
 -Reinforced Vest (protects player from their bombs and missiles)
 -Spear of Justice (upgradeable)
 -(yet another) Gaster Blaster minion
 -Item magnets!
 -Healing/Mana Stone (restores health & mana, can be used multiple times)
 -Demon Crown and Red Crystal (from Cave Story)
 -Baseball Bats
 -Wormhole Mirror
 -Some building materials & decorations
 
 And some more! Go to the forum page for the full list.
